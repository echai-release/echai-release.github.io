2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our services, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, company information, and any content you input into our conversational AI platform. We also automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns when you access our services.

Audio Data: We do not store or retain audio recordings from calls or voice interactions. Any audio data processed during voice conversations is handled in real-time and is not saved to our systems.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process transactions, send you technical notices and support messages, and communicate with you about products, services, and promotional offers. We may also use your information to personalize your experience and to develop new features and functionality for our platform.

4. Authentication Cookies and Data Security

We use authentication cookies to manage your login sessions and ensure secure access to your account. These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our service and contain encrypted session identifiers that allow us to recognize you as a logged-in user.

Cookie Details:
• Purpose: Authentication cookies are used exclusively for user login verification and session management
• Duration: These cookies expire after 1 day (24 hours) of inactivity
• Essential Nature: These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our login system and cannot be disabled while using our services

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, regular security audits, and access controls to limit who can access your information.

5. Cookie Policy

Essential Cookies Only
EnterpriseChai uses only essential cookies that are necessary for the basic functionality of our website and services. We do not use any non-essential cookies such as analytics, marketing, or tracking cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use:
• Authentication Cookies: Required for login and session management (duration: 1 day)
• Security Cookies: Used to detect and prevent security threats

What We Don't Use:
• No analytics or performance cookies
• No advertising or marketing cookies
• No social media tracking cookies
• No third-party tracking technologies

Since we only use essential cookies, no consent is required, and these cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the basic operation of our services.
